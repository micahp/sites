Broaden your spectrum and bridge the gap

Prospective Sponsors

September 1, 2016

Dear Prospective Sponsor,

We are pleased to announce that The University of Texas at Austin will host the 40th Annual Big XII Conference on Black Student Government on February 16-18, 2017. We invite you to be a financial sponsor of this special occasion. Hosted by the UT Austin Black Student Alliance, this was the first—and is the largest—conference organized by and for African/American students. Most exciting is the fact that this will be the first time this conference is held on the UT Austin campus, as well as in the state of Texas.

The 2017 conference theme, "Making the Movement Matter," embodies the conference goal to foster a spirirt of intellectual, physical and spiritual development for African/African American students attending Big XII Conference and non-Big XII Conference will inspire, empower, and cultivate these students to be the best in and out of the classroom.

As a sponsor of this conference, you will directly impact the lives of more than 1,000 anticpated particpants. Please make sure your gift or pledge today by competeing the online commitment form here. For more information, contact Mrs. Brenda Burt at (512) 475-7971 or via email at princekwame@mail.utexas.edu. Thank you for your consideration and I look forward to partnering with you to make this conference a success.

Sincerely,
Brenda Burt
